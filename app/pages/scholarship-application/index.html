SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION FOR GHOST ARTISTS

Due by 2/25/25

GHOST has up to five (5) $100 scholarships available for artists that require financial assistance with their membership. These can be new, returning or emerging artists. There is a limit of one scholarship per artist and they are only eligible for this one time. If two or more artists are involved with making one product, they are eligible for only one scholarship in total. If two artists live in the same household but make different products and pay separate artist fees, then they are eligible for one scholarship per person.

The GHOST board will evaluate the applications and decide which ones to approve, based on the merit of the application answers. The board reserves the right to not fulfill all 5 scholarships if suitable artist candidates are not identified. If more than 5 suitable artists apply for these scholarships, then a random drawing from applicants will be held to award the scholarships.

Approved scholarships will be applied to your contract payment section via a coupon code that you would receive by 3/1/25. Apply this coupon code to your contract payment section. Be sure to submit your contract along with the rest of your artist fees by the due date of March 2.
